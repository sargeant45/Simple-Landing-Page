Hi! I'm Ethan Arterberry.

I'm a young designer and developer, who is heavily interested in language and the web.

Right now, I'm working on a project called EssayMaker by myself.

I like video games, programming, and good food.

Here's some stuff I've done.

EssayMaker

EssayMaker is a labour of love I've worked on for at least 4 years. I made it to fix the problem of essay organization: a thing everyone needs to know how to do but everyone hates. EssayMaker takes the clunkiness and the pain out of essay organizers and then some. OCD work in progress.

|

Sexydown

actually boring

Sexydown is a speedy code and markup printer for the browser. It can print over 117 languages, syntax highlighted, plus rendered Textile and Markdown. There are tons of features, including Gist support, file upload, and a bunch of URL parameters the user can define to change settings and output.

|

WordADay

WordADay is a randomized dictionary for your iDevice. I currently work on WordADay at LanguageCraft with Lucas Stueber.

WordADay has also been featured on The iMums and KATU News.

seventh grade insecurity

"seventh grade insecurity" is a short book I wrote as a sort of love letter to seventh grade humor. It was written in just under 4 weeks, and includes 7 chapters. It is offensive, funny, and over exaggerated. In other words, it is the embodiment of seventh grade humor. It exercised some of my design skills, and it was a joy to create.

|

HECK and TheGame

HECK: Know Your Weaknesses and TheGame are two "games" I developed to test new things in new programming languages. HECK was developed to test out CSS3 animations, HTML5 audio, and some JQuery, while TheGame was developed to try out random things in .NET C#, like writing to a Notepad window and opening websites.

/r/dogeinvasion

I did the design CSS stylesheets for the subreddit /r/dogeinvasion. They include some animations, some Photoshop designs I made, with some other things on top. They were really fun to make, and they were an exercise of CSS.

|

sentimood

"sentimood" was built as a minimal AFINN sentiment analyzer that easily works in the browser, under the fact that there is no other easy to use, free, and open-source sentiment analyzer that I know of written for the browser.

Broadcast

Broadcast is a small CoffeeScript library (around 38 lines of CoffeeScript) that adds Scratch's broadcasts to the browser.
