Hi! I'm Ethan Arterberry.

Here's some stuff I've done.

EssayMaker

EssayMaker is a science fair project I created to better organize the essay writing progress. Basically, it's a graphic essay organizer that runs in your browser or on your computer. It was a work in progress for about 4 years, as it went through many stages from Scratch to the final product, which was finished in 2014.

|

Sexydown

Sexydown is a speedy code and markup printer for the browser. It can print over 117 languages, syntax highlighted, plus rendered Textile and Markdown. There are tons of features, including Gist support, file upload, and a bunch of URL parameters the user can define to change settings and output.

|

WordADay

WordADay is a project I made for a local FLL team, the Lego Megos. It grabs a random word from a text file of the dictionary, and then displays it, giving you the option to define it or say it out loud. I created an iPhone (unreleased) app, a Windows program, and a website.

|

seventh grade insecurity

"seventh grade insecurity" is a short book I wrote as a sort of love letter to seventh grade humor. It was written in just under 4 weeks, and includes 7 chapters. It is offensive, funny, and over exaggerated. In other words, it is the embodiment of seventh grade humor. It exercised some of my design skills, and it was a joy to create.

|

HECK and TheGame

HECK: Know Your Weaknesses and TheGame are two "games" I developed to test new things in new programming languages. HECK was developed to test out CSS3 animations, HTML5 audio, and some JQuery, while TheGame was developed to try out random things in .NET C#, like writing to a Notepad window and opening websites.
